About Me

My name is Jonathan Xu and I am an NYU graduate with a bachelor's degree in Computer Science.
I am passionate about programming and I am constantly striving to grow my skills as a software developer.
Below are my recent projects. Feel free to look around or message me if you have any questions.

UFC Winning Predictions

Machine learning model that utilizes UFC match history and data from Kaggle to make predictions on the outcome of fights. Uses three separate models (logistic regression, support vector machines, neural networks) and uses the model with highest accuracy for final decision.

Object Tracker

Computer vision project employing image classification techniques to track an object's movements within a video. Pick a coordinate on the first frame that corresponds with an object and it will track the object until it leaves frame or video ends.

Roblox Terrain Generator

Leveraged machine noise to generate realistic and random terrain on an isometric grid. Environment will vary randomly as well, resulting in desert biomes, ocean biomes, etc.
